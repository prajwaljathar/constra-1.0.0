Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Site Engineer to join our team and oversee construction activities at various project sites.

Responsibilities:

  • Supervise and coordinate construction activities at project sites
  • Ensure compliance with project specifications, design drawings, and safety regulations
  • Coordinate with subcontractors, vendors, and project stakeholders
  • Manage resources, materials, and equipment at the site
  • Prepare daily progress reports and maintain site documentation
  • Conduct quality checks and implement quality control measures
  • Address and resolve site-related issues promptly

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or equivalent
  • 3+ years of experience in construction site management
  • Proficiency in reading and interpreting construction drawings
  • Knowledge of construction methods, materials, and quality standards
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills
  • Ability to work in a team and manage multiple priorities

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled Structural Engineer to design and analyze structural systems for our building and infrastructure projects.

Responsibilities:

  • Design structural elements and systems for buildings and infrastructure projects
  • Perform structural analysis using advanced software tools
  • Ensure designs comply with building codes, standards, and specifications
  • Prepare structural drawings and calculation reports
  • Conduct site visits to assess structural conditions and requirements
  • Collaborate with architects, MEP engineers, and other design professionals
  • Review shop drawings and respond to RFIs during construction

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Structural Engineering
  • 5+ years of experience in structural design for buildings
  • Proficiency in structural analysis software (ETABS, STAAD.Pro, etc.)
  • Knowledge of concrete, steel, and timber structural design
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
  • Professional engineering license preferred
